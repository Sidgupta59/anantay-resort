About

Welcome to Anantay – A Boutique Homestay in Tirthan Valley Tucked away in the heart of Himachal, Anantay offers a luxurious mountain retreat with the most breathtaking view of the Tirthan Valley – where majestic peaks meet the river winding through the foothills. Designed for those who appreciate the finer things, our boutique homestay blends natural beauty with modern comfort and understated elegance. Savor a specially curated menu crafted for our guests, featuring the best of local and seasonal flavors. Every meal is a culinary experience, served with warmth and attention to detail. Whether you’re relaxing in our thoughtfully styled spaces or exploring the valley’s pristine beauty, Anantay is your sanctuary in the mountains. Come for the view. Stay for the experience.
